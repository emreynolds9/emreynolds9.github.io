About me

I grew up and got my degree in economics in Fort Myers, Florida. Currently, I work at an academic non-profit in Arlington, Virginia. I have always loved math and logic, and had a strong hunch that I would like coding, so I took the plunge and enrolled in a data analytics boot camp. Thankfully, I ended up really liking coding and am incredibly excited to learn more.

When I am not coding or making endless tweaks to this website, I enjoy playing piano, attempting to play my accordion, going to concerts, and reading about economics and political philosophy.

My miscellaneous writings have been aggregated on an infrequently updated blog.
